# What's Happening Around Me? 🌍

"What's Happening Around Me?" is a simple, fast application designed to explain local environmental conditions and simulate future impacts in engaging, youth-friendly language. 

Instead of overwhelming users with complex charts, raw data, or confusing scientific jargon, this app translates real-time environmental metrics into relatable analogies that anyone can understand, and projects future trajectories using AI modeling.

## 1. Current Focus (Real-time Explainer)

- **Enter Your Location**: Simply type in where you are (e.g., "New York", "London", "Tokyo").
- **Pick Your Concern**: Select the environmental issue you care about most right now, such as Air Pollution, Heat Waves, or Flooding.
- **Get an Explanation**: The app pulls real-time, hyper-local data (like Temperature, Air Quality Index, Humidity, and Wind Speed) and uses AI to generate a short, conversational summary. For example, instead of just saying "AQI 165", it might tell you: *"That's like breathing smoke from a campfire all day."*

## 2. Future Simulator (Impact Visualization)

- **Set the Scene**: Enter your Current Age, your target Future Age, and your City.
- **Pick a Scenario & Trajectory**: Choose an environmental threat (like "Rising Sea Levels") and use the custom slider to select a world trajectory (from "Cleaner Future" to an extreme "+2°C World").
- **Glimpse Your Future**: The app uses OpenAI to generate projected numerical metrics (like expected Summer Highs, or Unhealthy AQI Days per year) and writes a 3-sentence immersive story of what your daily life will consist of physically in that exact future year.

The goal is to make environmental awareness accessible, simple, and immediately understandable for everyone.
